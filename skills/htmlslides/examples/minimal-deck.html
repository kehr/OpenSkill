Setup

What I changed

  • Laptop in another room, all notifications off.
  • One A5 notebook, one fountain pen, one ink bottle.
  • No structured planner -- only blank pages.
  • A small kitchen timer set to 50 minutes per session.

Notes I kept

Three kinds of pages

Morning

Stream of consciousness, no editing. Page filled in 18 minutes on average.

Working

Lists and diagrams. Far slower than typed -- but I remembered what was on them.

Evening

One paragraph summarizing the day. Honest because it was for me.
